Monthly Averages

Shows average conditions for each month over the stations entire data set.

Computed values are:

  • Average High
  • Daily Mean
  • Average Low
  • Average Rain
  • Average Rain Days (days where rainfall >= 1.0mm)
  • Average Humidity
  • Average Wind Speed
  • Average Gale Days (days where average wind is >= 63km/h)

This report may be useful for obtaining the Normal Rainfall and Mean Temperature parameters for the NOAA Year Summary report where official figures for your location are unavailable.
