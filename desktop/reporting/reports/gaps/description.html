Data Gaps

This report displays all gaps in the weather stations historical record along with their label if there is one.

A gap is anywhere where the time between two consecutive samples is twice the sample interval or more. This indicates that at least one sample that should have been produced by the weather station was not or was not recorded.

Possible causes for gaps include:

  • Data logger experienced an error downloading one or more samples
  • Weather-Push hasn't replicated one or more samples yet
  • Weather station failed to produce one or more samples due to being offline (no power, in setup mode, etc)
  • Data deleted from the database
