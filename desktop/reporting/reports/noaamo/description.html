NOAA Month Summary

This report summarises the weather for the month. It supports both metric and US units and can calculate heating and cooling degree days. It includes the following data for each day:

  • Mean temperature
  • High Temperature & Time
  • Low Temperature & Time
  • Heating Degree-Days
  • Cooling Degree-Days
  • Rainfall
  • Average Wind Speed
  • High Wind Speed & Time
  • Dominant Wind Direction
The entire month is summarised in the final line at the bottom of the table. The time column for this line is the day number where the high or low for the month occurred.

At the bottom of the report are counts where the days:

  • Maximum temperature was 32°C (90°F) or higher
  • Maximum temperature was 0°C (32°F) or lower
  • Minimum temperature was 0°C (32°F) or lower
  • Minimum temperature was -18°C (0°F) or lower
  • Where rain exceeded 0.2mm (0.01"), 2mm (0.1") or 20mm (1")
