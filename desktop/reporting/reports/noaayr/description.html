NOAA Year Summary

This report summarises the weather for the year. It supports both metric and US units and can calculate heating and cooling degree days. It includes the following data for each month divided into three tables:

Temperature

  • Mean high and low
  • Mean
  • Departure from normal mean temperature for the month
  • Heating Degree-Days
  • Cooling Degree-Days
  • High temperature & date
  • Low temperature & date
  • Number of days where the high was 32°C (90°F) or higher
  • Number of days where the high was 0°C (32°F) or lower
  • Number of days where the low was 0°C (32°F) or lower
  • Number of days where the low was -18°C (0°F) or lower

Rainfall

  • Total
  • Departure from normal rainfall for the month
  • Highest observed rainfall for the month & date
  • Days where rain exceeded 0.2mm (0.01"), 2mm (0.1") or 20mm (1")

Wind Speed

  • Average wind speed
  • High wind speed & date
  • Dominant Wind Direction

The entire year is summarised at the bottom of each table with the month name appearing in the high and low date columns naming the month where that record appeared.
