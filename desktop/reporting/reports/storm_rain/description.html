Storm Rain

Shows all storm rain episodes during the chosen timespan.

A storm is defined as:

  • Starting with 0.4mm of rain in a 24 hour period
  • Ending with 0mm of rain in a 24 hour period

The time filter on this report shows all storms starting within the chosen timespan. All rainfall for the storm is shown including rain that falls outside the chosen timespan. Storms occuring during the timespan with a start time that falls before the chosen timespan will not be shown.

Each storm rain episode includes a link to view a plot of the storm
