Sunrise & Sunset

Calculates sun rise and set times for your chosen location and time zone as well as day length and solar noon time. Daylight savings will be handled automatically for the time zone chosen.

A simple plain-text output similar to that provided by WeatherLink is also generated. Differences from the WeatherLink version are a DD/MM/YY date format instead of MM/DD/YY and the time zone is reported under its IANA time zone database name. Daylight savings is always automatic.
