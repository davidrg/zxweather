Daily Data

The following datasets are available for each day:

Format Link Description
Google DataTable JSON datatable/samples.json All outdoor samples for the day. Should be around 288 records.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/hourly_rainfall.json Total rainfall for each hour in the day
Google DataTable JSON datatable/7day_hourly_rainfall.json Total rainfall for each hour in the past seven days.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/7day_samples.json Every outdoor sample over the past seven days. Should be around 2016 records.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/7day_30m_avg_samples.json Averaged outdoor samples every 30 minutes for the past 7 days.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/indoor_samples.json All indoor samples for the day. Should be around 288 records.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/7day_indoor_samples.json Every indoor sample over the past seven days. Should be around 2016 records.
Google DataTable JSON datatable/7day_30m_avg_indoor_samples.json Averaged indoor samples every 30 minutes for the past 7 days.
